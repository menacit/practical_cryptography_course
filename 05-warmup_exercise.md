## Exercise: Historic fails
Participants are split into four or more groups.  
Each group will be assigned an historic event
that involved use or abuse of cryptography.

Group members should research and discuss:
- What happend?
- What were the consequences?
- What lessons may be learned?
  
After presentation, send notes to
